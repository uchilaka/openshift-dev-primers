Getting started with Office 365 Teams

We've prepared a short video showing the steps to get started with Office 365 Teams.

Before setting up Teams on your mobile device, be sure you've completed the following steps:

  • Check your inbox for your Office 365 tenant setup email sent on 11/02 at about 4pm. The email has the details for access to the test tenant
  • Visit office.com to confirm access to your account
  • Enter firstname.lastname@adshackathon.onmicrosoft.com for your username
  • Enter the temporary password from your setup email (above)
When you complete your login, you should arrive at your O365 dashboard, showing the Office Apps.
